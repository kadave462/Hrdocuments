What is Ejabo HR?
Ejabo HR is a cloud-based Human Resource Management System (HRMS) built on a microservices architecture. It enables companies to manage their entire employee lifecycle — from onboarding to payroll, attendance, leave, training, and termination — through a single unified platform.
User Roles Overview

🔴 Super Admin SUPER

Platform-level administrator. Creates and manages company accounts, global roles, permissions, business types, and subscription plans. Has visibility across all companies on the platform.

🟠 Company Admin ADMIN

Top-level user within a company. Registers the company, creates HR users and managers, configures company-wide settings (payroll, leave types, grades, shifts), and views company statistics.

🔵 HR Manager / HR User HR

Manages day-to-day HR operations — employee records, attendance, leave requests, payroll processing, trainings, suspensions, and terminations. Generates all reports.

🟢 Employee EMP

Self-service access — applies for leave, views personal attendance records and payslips, checks schedule, updates personal profile and bank details.

👤 Employee Management
The Employee module is the central hub of Ejabo HR. It covers the full employee lifecycle from hire to exit. HR users can create, search, view, and manage all employee records.

Adding an Employee HR ADMIN

1

Navigate to All Employees

Go to Employees → All Employees. Click the Add Employee button.

2

Fill in Personal Details

Enter first name, last name, email, phone, national ID, date of birth, gender, nationality, and marital status.

3

Add Employment Details

Select position, department, grade/level, reporting manager, and start date. Assign an employee number (or let the system auto-generate one based on your settings).

4

Upload Documents

Upload required documents (national ID, contract, certificates) using the file upload section. Supported document types are configured in Settings → Document Types.

5

Save & Create Account

Save the employee. The system automatically creates a user account and sends login credentials to the employee's registered email.

Bulk Employee Import
To add multiple employees at once, use the Bulk Create option. Download the provided template, fill in employee data, and upload the file. The system will process all records simultaneously.

🕐 Attendance Management
Ejabo HR supports multiple attendance recording methods: manual entry, QR code scanning, and biometric attendance machines. All methods feed into a unified attendance record.

Recording Attendance HR

Manual Entry

Go to Attendance → Attendance Sheet. Select the employee, date, check-in time, and check-out time. Save the record. Useful for corrections or retroactive entries.

QR Code Scanning

Employees scan their personal QR code at check-in/check-out terminals. The system automatically records the timestamp linked to their profile.

Biometric Machines

Configured attendance machines (see Attendance Machines module) push records automatically. No manual entry needed — records sync in real time.

Bulk Upload

Upload a CSV/Excel file with multiple attendance records at once. Use for bulk corrections or importing data from external sources.

My Attendance EMP

Employees navigate to Attendance → My Attendance to view their personal attendance history filtered by month. Records show check-in time, check-out time, hours worked, and status.

Handling Absent / Missed Check-ins HR

1

View Absent Today

Go to Attendance → Absent Today to see employees who have not checked in for the current day.

2

Process Pending Sign-Ins

Go to Attendance Sheet and look for pending sign-in records. HR can approve, adjust, or reject pending records.

3

Manual Correction

If an employee forgot to check in or out, HR can create a manual attendance record on their behalf with the correct times.

🌴 Leave Management
Ejabo HR supports multiple leave types (annual, sick, maternity, etc.), configurable approval workflows, and real-time leave balance tracking per employee.

2

3

Pick start date and end date. Add a reason or supporting comment. The system automatically calculates the number of working days for the period.

4

Submit for Approval

Click Submit. Your request enters a Pending state and is sent to your designated approver(s).

5

Track Your Request

Monitor status under Leave → Pending Leaves, Approved Leaves, or Rejected Leaves tabs.

💰 Payroll Management
Ejabo HR automates salary calculations including base salary, allowances, benefits, RSSB contributions, PAYE tax, maternity deductions, and other configured deductions. Results are output as payslips and bank salary reports.

Understanding Payroll Components

Benefits / Allowances

Fixed or variable additions to base salary: housing allowance, transport, medical, etc. Configured in Payroll → Benefits.

Deductions

Reductions from gross pay: loans, absences, disciplinary fines. Configured in Payroll → Deductions.

PAYE (Tax)

Pay As You Earn income tax brackets configured under Payroll → PAYE. Automatically applied during payroll processing.

RSSB

Rwanda Social Security Board contributions (employer + employee portions) — automatically calculated on gross salary.

Maternity Deduction

Tracked separately for maternity-related payroll adjustments. Viewable in the payroll summary per employee.

Work Hour Rates

Hourly rates for overtime and part-time work configured in Payroll → Work Hour Settings.

Processing Payroll HR ADMIN

1

Navigate to Payroll

Go to Payroll → Payroll. Select the payroll period (month/year).

2

Initiate Processing

Click Process Payroll. The system calculates gross salary, applies all benefits, deductions, taxes, and RSSB for each active employee.

3

Review Draft

Payroll enters DRAFT status. Review individual payslips for accuracy — check benefits totals, deductions, and net pay figures.

4

Approve Payroll

If everything is correct, click Approve. Status changes to APPROVED. This locks the figures for the period.

5

Generate Reports

Export individual payslips as PDF, or the full company payroll as Excel. The Bank Salary Report lists net pay per employee for bank disbursement.

Cancelling Payroll
A payroll in DRAFT status can be cancelled if corrections are needed. APPROVED payrolls require admin-level action to reverse. Always verify draft figures before approving.

Viewing Payslips EMP

Employees can access their own payslips at Employees → Me under the Payroll tab. Each payslip shows gross salary, itemized benefits, all deductions, tax calculations (PAYE + RSSB), and final net pay. Payslips can be downloaded as PDF.

Configuring Benefits & Services ADMIN

Company Services

  • Go to Settings → Services
  • Create service categories (e.g., "Medical Plan", "Housing")
  • Services are linked to specific benefit types

Benefits

  • Go to Payroll → Benefits
  • Create benefit items under each service
  • Set fixed amount, percentage of salary, or custom formula

Deductions

  • Go to Payroll → Deductions
  • Create deduction types with names and calculation rules
  • Assign to individual employees as needed

PAYE Brackets

  • Go to Payroll → PAYE
  • Enter income bands and corresponding tax rates
  • System uses these during payroll processing
📄 Contracts, Suspensions & Terminations
The Contract Manager module handles the entire employment contract lifecycle, including change requests, suspensions, warnings, and terminations — all with approval workflows.

Managing Contracts HR ADMIN

1

View Contracts

Go to Contract Manager → View Contract to see all employee contracts, their types (permanent, fixed-term), start/end dates, and current status.

2

Create / Update a Contract

Open an employee's profile, go to the Contract tab. Fill in contract type, start date, end date (if applicable), and salary. Upload the signed contract document.

3

Request Contract Changes

To modify a contract (e.g., salary revision, position change), submit a Contract Change Request. This enters an approval queue before being applied to the employee record.

4

Approve / Reject Change Request

Approvers review pending contract changes. Once approved, the employee's contract is automatically updated. Rejected requests are logged with the reason.

Employee Suspensions HR ADMIN

1

Submit Suspension Request

Navigate to Contract Manager → Contract Suspension. Select the employee, provide the suspension reason, start date, and expected duration.

2

Approval Workflow

The request goes to the configured approver. They can approve or reject with comments. Once approved, the employee's status changes to Suspended.

3

Impact on Payroll

Suspended employees are excluded from payroll processing for the suspension period. Salary is not calculated until their status returns to active.

Employee Warnings HR MGR

Navigate to Contract Manager → Warning to issue disciplinary warnings to employees. Each warning record includes type, date, description, and issuing manager. Warning history is visible on the employee's profile and contributes to termination evidence if needed.

Employee Terminations HR ADMIN

1

Submit Termination Request

Navigate to Settings → Termination or from the employee's profile. Select termination type (resignation, dismissal, end of contract), effective date, and reason.

2

Approval Workflow

The request enters an approval queue. Approvers review and can approve or reject. Once approved, the employee's status changes to Terminated.

3

Final Settlement

After termination approval, process the final payroll for the employee — including any outstanding salary, accrued leave payout (Pécule de Vacances), and any applicable severance.

4

Close Employee Account

The employee's user account is deactivated. Their records remain accessible for historical reporting and compliance purposes.

Irreversible Action Warning
Approved terminations are very difficult to reverse. Ensure all approvals, documentation, and final payroll are completed correctly before confirming a termination request.

🔐 Roles & Permissions
Access to every feature in Ejabo HR is governed by a permission assigned to a role. Roles are assigned to users. Only Admin-level users should manage roles and permissions to avoid unintended access.

Managing Roles ADMIN

1

Navigate to Users → Roles

Go to Users → Roles. You'll see a list of all existing roles for your company.

2

Create a New Role

Click Add Role. Enter the role name (e.g., "HR Manager", "Finance Lead"). Each role is a named collection of permissions.

3

Assign Permissions to the Role

After creating the role, go to Users → Permissions. Select permissions (e.g., "can view payroll", "can approve leave") and assign them to the role.

4

Assign Role to Employee

Open the employee's profile and update their assigned role. Changes take effect at next login.

5

Update an Existing Role

Go to Users → Roles, find the role, and click Edit. Modify permissions as needed. All users with that role inherit the change immediately.

Principle of Least Privilege
Assign only the permissions each role genuinely needs. Avoid giving all employees admin-level access. Review role assignments periodically, especially when an employee changes position.
